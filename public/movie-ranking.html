The Best Movies According to Adesh

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Wolf of Wall Street

This is my favorite crime/comedy movie. Leonardio DiCaprio is my favorite actor.

The Fast and the Furious: Tokyo Drift

Favorite racing movie, I am a car guy. Cars were really nice, acting was great and action was even better.

Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire

Best movie out of the series in my opinion. Loved the idea of the triwizard tournament.
